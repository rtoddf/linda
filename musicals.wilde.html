This trio of poignant fairy tales by Oscar Wilde includes The Happy Prince, The Selfish Giant and The Star-Child. Musical piano themes heighten the plots and underscore the varied emotions of the characters. This is a stunning  adaptation of truly classic fables. You can choose to license some or all of the fables.

Cast Requirements

The Selfish Giant:

  • Giant (double as Voice Over 1)
  • Snow (double as Child 2 & Voice Over 3)
  • Frost (double as Child 3 & Voice Over 5)
  • Hail (double as Child 4, Voice Over 4 & Bird 2)
  • North Wind (double as Child 5, Voice Over 2 & Bird 1)
  • Child 1 (double as Voice Over 6)

The Happy Prince:

  • Prince
  • Swallow
  • Beggar (and others)
  • Mother (and others)
  • Teacher (and others)
  • Child (and Angel)

The Star-Child:

  • Mole (and others)
  • Linnet (and others)
  • Blind Man (and others)
  • Beggar Woman (and others)
  • Magician (and others)
  • Star-Child
