We Three Kings reveals the true identities of the Magi, why they traveled to Bethlehem that first Christmas, and the symbolism of their gifts. Gabriel announces the impending arrival of the New Messiah to Balthasar who then summons Gaspar and Melchior to join him on the journey to where the baby will be born. The Magi encounter bedouins who inform them of King Herod's evil plan to destroy any new usurpers to his kingdom and realize that they must help the Holy Family. This spiritually uplifting Christmas adventure is laced with humor yet steeped in theological and historical information, and punctuated wih beautiful music. Sure to become a time-honored seasonal tradition.
