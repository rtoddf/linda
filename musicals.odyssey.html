After twenty years of war and fantastic adventures, Odysseus faces his greatest challenge upon his return to Ithaca. He must reunite with his wife, who is beset by villains and usurpers, come to know his son whom he's never seen, and regain control of his country. Powerful and emotional music helps to heighten the tension and reveal the innermost thoughts of Odyssey's characters. Truly a theatrical experience for the entire family.
