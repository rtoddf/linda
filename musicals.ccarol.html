This tried and true version of A Christmas Carol maintains the content and intent of the original classic by Charles Dickens. We meet Marley, the Ghosts of Christmas Past, Present and Future, the Cratchits and poor Tiny Tim, as well as members of Scrooge's life, both in his past and present. The spirited music brings great fun to its characters as we do the Jolly Jig with the Fezziwigs and meet the colorful characters of Ol' Joe, the Laundress and the Charwoman. Its beautiful ballads evoke tears of emotion, both sad and happy. This time honored family show has proven to be a holiday tradition for thousands.
