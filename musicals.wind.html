Wind in the Willows is a musical adaptation of Kenneth Grahame’s beloved work which tells the story of the valiant friendship between the good-hearted Water Rat, the innocent and curious Mole, and the sensible and practical Badger. Their good friend is the arrogant, wily, and slightly crazed Mr. Toad who is obsessive and given to sudden whims. He procures a motor car and drives recklessly about the countryside, creating all kinds of havoc and schemes. Toad gets his comeuppance when he lands in jail, but his winsome ways work for him and he manages to escape. This fanciful tale also includes some gangster type weasels and ferrets, and is great fun for the entire family.
